Genius Sheets Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy relates to information collected online and by Genius Sheets Inc. ("Genius Sheets" "we" or "us" or "our") through your use of the Genius Sheets website at geniussheets.com and any subdomains thereof (the "Site"), and the services, features, and information available on the Site, including the Genius Sheets add-in and associated software.

This policy also addresses our practices and obligations under the General Data Protection Regulation 2016/679 ("GDPR") for users in the European Economic Area and United Kingdom. When Genius Sheets receives personal information about users through their Organization, Genius Sheets acts as a processor and the Organization acts as the controller.

"Using" and "processing" information includes cookies, statistical analysis, collecting, storing, evaluating, modifying, deleting, combining, disclosing, and transferring information.

Information We Collect

Personally Identifiable Information

We may collect personally identifiable information including your name, email address, employer, username, password, and billing/credit card information collected by our Payment Services Provider.

Non-Personally Identifiable Information

Non-personally identifiable information is information, any single item of which, by itself, cannot be used to identify or contact you. This includes demographic data, browser types, device identifiers, URLs, browser language, visit dates and times, and domain names.

How We Collect Information

Registration and Service Use

Users may need to establish accounts providing name, email, employer, username, password, and payment information.

Cookies

Cookies are small text files placed on computers or devices to identify browsers and track activities. They personalize experiences, assist with service use, and monitor usage patterns. Users can reject cookies, though some service features may not work properly. Most browsers allow cookie management through help sections. Third-party service platforms may place their own cookies. Google Analytics is a primary third-party platform used.

Action Tags

Action tags (also known as web beacons or gif tags) track website usage like page views and are invisible to users.

Log Files

Internet log files record IP addresses, browser types, domain names, and anonymous statistical data used for trend analysis and demographic information gathering.

Children's Privacy

The Service is not designed for or directed to children under the age of 13. We do not collect personally identifiable information from any person we actually know is under the age of 13.

How We Use Your Information

Legal Basis Under GDPR

Processing bases include user consent (withdrawable anytime), contract performance necessity, and legitimate interests in providing and marketing the Service.

Uses

We use your information to:

  • Provide the Service and process transactions
  • Understand Service users
  • Conduct internal operations and improvements
  • Contact you for customer service and billing
  • Send marketing communications (unless you opt out)
  • Send welcoming emails with account details
  • Send newsletters and targeted communications
  • Respond to your inquiries
  • Send service announcements and updates
  • Perform statistical analysis and evaluate Service improvements

You will not be able to unsubscribe from service announcements and updates as they contain important information relevant to your use of the Service.

Non-personally identifiable information may be provided to third parties, including analytics providers and commercial partners. Google Analytics is specifically used for its data reporting features.

Personally identifiable information collected on the Service may be transferred from time to time to our offices or personnel, or to third parties, located throughout the world. Whenever we transfer your personal data to third parties located in an Inadequate Jurisdiction, we ensure a similar degree of protection is afforded to it.

Data Retention

We will only retain your personally identifiable information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes for which we collected it. We consider the amount, nature, and sensitivity of the information, potential harm risks, processing purposes, alternative means, and legal requirements when determining retention periods.

Your Rights and Choices

General

You generally must "opt in" for non-standard uses and may "opt out" of marketing communications by following the directions in those communications or by contacting privacy@geniussheets.com.

GDPR Rights

Under certain circumstances, you may:

  • Request access to your personal information (subject access request)
  • Request correction of incomplete or inaccurate information
  • Request erasure of your personal information
  • Object to processing based on legitimate interest
  • Request processing restrictions

Requests should be directed to privacy@geniussheets.com.

You should be aware that it may not be technologically possible to remove each and every record of the information you have provided to us from our system. We use commercially reasonable efforts to update, correct, change, or delete information in actively used databases and readily searchable media.

Do Not Track

We take no action in response to automated Do Not Track requests. However, if you wish to stop such tracking, please contact us with your request.

Changes to This Policy

We may revise this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will not make changes that result in significant additional uses or disclosures of your personally identifiable information without allowing you to "opt in" to such changes. Users should check this page periodically and contact us immediately if changes are unacceptable.
