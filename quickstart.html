Quickstart Guide

Get up and running with Genius Sheets in just a few minutes.

1

Download the App

Download the Genius Sheets add-in from either the Google Workspace Marketplace or the Microsoft Excel Store. For Google Sheets, go to Extensions > Add-ons > Get Add-ons.

2

Create an Account

Sign up for a free account on the Genius Sheets website. No credit card required for your two-week free trial.

Sign Up Free
3

Connect Your QuickBooks Account

Once an account has been created, on the dashboard page of the website select the "Connect To QuickBooks" button and complete the QuickBooks authentication process.

4

Log Into the Add-In

Open the Genius Sheets add-in in your spreadsheet and log in using your account credentials. You're ready to start pulling data.

Key Features

Text To Reports

Generate reports using simple English descriptions of the report type and time period you need. Just describe what you want and Genius Sheets builds the report for you.

Live Data Connection

Enable real-time data synchronization between your spreadsheets and QuickBooks. Your reports stay current with the latest financial data.

Custom Formulas

Build reports using three core formulas:

  • GS.IS — Income Statement
  • GS.BS — Balance Sheet
  • GS.CF — Cash Flow Statement

Formula syntax: =GS.IS("Category", "Start Date", "End Date", "Company Name", "Filter")

Consolidated Reporting

Build customized reports across multiple QuickBooks entities using company nicknames and abbreviations.

Nightly Refresh

Google Sheets users can enable automatic nightly data synchronization so reports are always up to date when you open them.

Formula Filtering

Filter formula results by Class, Customer, Product/Service, Location, Department, or Vendor to get exactly the data you need.

List Categories

Use the "List Categories" menu option to view the exact names of the categories, including account numbers, from your QuickBooks Online account.

Transaction-Level Drill-Down

View the underlying transaction details for any formula cell to see exactly where the numbers come from.
